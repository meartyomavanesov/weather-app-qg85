The Weather App is a cutting-edge mobile application designed to provide users with accurate and real-time weather information tailored to their specific location. Targeting outdoor enthusiasts, travelers, and anyone who wants to stay informed about weather conditions, the app allows users to access current weather data, hourly and daily forecasts, and severe weather alerts. Users can customize their experience by setting preferred locations, receiving notifications for significant weather changes, and viewing detailed information such as temperature, humidity, wind speed, and precipitation levels. By equipping users with timely and relevant weather updates, the app helps them make informed decisions about their daily activities, travel plans, and outdoor events, ultimately enhancing their overall experience and safety.

- Real-time weather updates for current conditions
- Hourly and daily forecasts for planning ahead
- Severe weather alerts to keep users informed of dangerous conditions
- Customizable location settings for personalized weather information
- Detailed weather metrics including temperature, humidity, and wind speed
- User-friendly interface for easy navigation and quick access
- Notifications for significant weather changes to stay prepared
- Integration with maps for visual representation of weather patterns
